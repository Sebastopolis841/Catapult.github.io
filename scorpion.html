Breif Description

Scorpion catapults (also called a scorpio) were different from a regular catapults. Unlike other catapults, it was used to attack people instead of fortifications like a sort of crossbow.

Uses and Numbers

A scorpion catapult had 2 functions. It can be used for precision shooting, where it can accurately hit any target within 100 meters, or parabolic shooting where it can hit a target within 400 meters and shoot 3 to 4 shots per minute. The precision shooting had a signigicantly slower rate of fire from the parabolic shooting. A scorpion bolt was heavy and fast enough that they can pierce shields.

Building and Materials

A scorpion catapult was made up of a wooden or metal base and some ropes in a metal box which acted as springs.
